Pepper

Black Pepper are dried berries of a perennial tropical climbing vine which is native to the evergreen hill forests located in the state of Kerala in India. Pepper are small green and red coloured berries which turn black on drying. Black Pepper which have a sharp strong aroma and woody undertones.

HOW TO STORE

Black Pepper should be stored in a cool airtight containers away from direct sunlight.
